Our Story

A Neighborhood Cafe
with a Latin Twist

Tucked into a charming brick corner on Princess Street, Folks Cafe has become one of Wilmington's most beloved morning spots. We're known for our handcrafted empanadas alongside classic American breakfast — a combo that keeps our regulars coming back every week.

Everything made fresh daily — no shortcuts, no shortcuts, no shortcuts.

Dog-friendly patio so your whole family can join the morning ritual.

A cozy, welcoming space beloved by locals, students, and visitors alike.
